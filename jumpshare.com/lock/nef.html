Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it safe to use this tool to lock my NEF file?

    Your NEF file and its password are secured using AES-256 military-grade encryption. We will keep your file on our servers for 24 hours, after which it will be automatically deleted. To keep your files online indefinitely, consider signing up for a free Jumpshare account.

  • Will my NEF file remain password protected after I download it?

    No. Our tool keeps your file safe behind a password protection prompt in our powerful online file viewer, which offers additional security and privacy features, available to Jumpshare registrants. Password protection is not applied to the file itself.

  • Does the tool have any usage limitations?

    You can upload just one NEF file at a time with this free tool and it must not exceed the maximum file size of 100MB. You're limited to a total of 3 NEF file uploads per day. To upload more NEF files or ones larger than 100MB, you may sign up for a free account.

  • I have other questions about your password protection tool!

    No problem! We have a 24/7 support team to assist you with password protecting files online. Just shoot us a message at hello@jumpshare.com and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
