Free Video Embedding

Jumpshare lets you embed your videos anywhere for free. All you need to embed your videos is to upload them to our online library first. Once uploaded, open the video on Jumpshare and click Share from the top-right corner and click on the Embed tab. From there, you can adjust the embedding to be responsive or of a fixed size. You can set the dimensions however you want. Copy the embedding code and use it on your website. A preview is available to see how the embedding will look like. If you add a CTA (Call-to-action) button to the video, it will also be visible to viewers.

Responsive Embedding

You can select a responsive option for embedding, which allows videos to scale and fit the page of any width. It’s helpful when playing video on a desktop, mobile, or tablet, you won’t have to worry about size adjustments.

Fixed Embedding

You can set a fixed size of the embedded video to your liking to give the best experience to your viewers. When changing the width of the video, the height will be adjusted accordingly preserving the aspect ratio.

Video Preview

Get a clear idea of how your video will look after embedding with our quick preview. The preview lets you view how your video will look like when embedded. If you have added a CTA button, that shows in the preview as well.

Easy Embedding

Our embedding code is super-flexible. You can add it anywhere on your page without worrying about breaking the website. There is nothing else that you have to insert to make it work.

Track Views

You can track how many people have viewed your embedded video, from where, and when. Jumpshare provides in-depth analytics to keep you informed on how your videos are performing.

Password Protection

Jumpshare provides advanced options that put you in full control over your files. You can password protect your embedded videos so only those with a password can view them.

How to Embed Videos

1.Upload Video

Visit your Jumpshare account and upload a video by clicking the Upload button in the library. You can also drag & drop your videos.

2.Get Embedding Code

Open the video, click Share option and then head over to the Embed tab. Set the resolution to responsive or fixed size and click the Copy Code button.

3.Add to page

Open the page you want to embed the video in with a HTML editor. Decide where you want to add the video and press CTRL+V to paste the code.
