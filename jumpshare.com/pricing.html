    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the difference between the Plus and Business plan?

    The Plus plan is for individuals and small teams who want to get the most out of Jumpshare. It has all the features you need to work better. The Business plan is for growing teams who want to be more productive. It comes with better control over the team members in the Workspace through privacy controls, organization-wide policies, and team activity stream.

    Can I have fewer than 3 members in the Business plan?

    The Business plan starts from 3 members minimum. You can upgrade to the Business plan even when your Workspace has less than 3 members. The extra seats can be reserved for contractors or clients with whom you want to collaborate on projects. If youâ€™re an individual or have less than 3 members in your Workspace and you donâ€™t want the Business features, you can upgrade to the Plus plan.

    Can I change/cancel my plan anytime?

    Absolutely! You can move between plans or cancel the plan anytime during your subscription period. Your files will remain unaffected.

    Can I try the Business plan for free?

    Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial so you can take the Jumpshare Business plan for a spin. You're not charged during this period.

    What happens at the end of my Business trial?

    When your 14-day trial ends, you'll automatically be charged for the subscription based on the number of members in your Workspace. If you cancel before your trial ends, you will not be charged.
