Test Your Microphone in Seconds

Test your internal and external microphones with our free online mic test tool. Our tool can test all types of mics and doesn't require you to download or install any additional software on your Windows, Mac, or Linux system. All you need is a modern web browser, such as Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.

View Audio Waveform

See what your mic is capturing with our live audio visualizer. Instantly know if your audio input is working.

Listen With Online Playback

Assess the sound quality of your microphone. The test records 30 seconds of input from your mic, which you can then listen to right within your browser. This recording isn't saved to our servers.

Download Your Recording

Download 30 seconds of recorded audio for offline troubleshooting. Install the Jumpshare desktop app for more recording time and an online library of your own to store your recordings.

How to Test Your Mic Online

  • 1

    Click the Start Mic Test button at the top of the page. When your browser requests access to your microphone for this page, click Allow.

  • 2

    Select the mic you'd like to test from the dropdown in the testing area at the top and click the Start Mic Test button.

  • 3

    Wait for the recording to end or hit the End Test button at any point to listen to your recording.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it safe to use this tool?

    Yes! All processing is done on your side and we do not save your recording on our servers.

  • I refreshed the page but didn't see a prompt to enable mic access. What do I do?

    It seems you may have blocked mic access for this page. You'll have to change the permissions for the page in your browser. On Google Chrome, you can do this by clicking on the Settings icon in the left corner of the address bar and switching the Microphone option on from the pop-up.

  • Why can't I see my microphone in the drop-down menu at the top?

    Your microphone may be disconnected or configured improperly. If you have an external microphone, check that it is connected properly. If you're on Windows, try reinstalling the proper audio drivers for your system.
